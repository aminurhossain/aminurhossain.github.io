About Me

Researcher, scientist, and lifelong learner

My Story

I'm a PhD student at CSRE, IIT Bombay, advised by Prof. Biplab Banerjee. My research focuses on Deep Learning, Computer Vision for Remote Sensing, Generative AI, and Multi-Modal Models for Remote Sensing applications.

I am passionate about leveraging advanced machine learning models to solve complex problems in remote sensing and related fields. I also enjoy sharing knowledge through teaching and writing about data science and mathematics.

Currently, I am working as a Scientist-SE at the Space Applications Centre, ISRO, Ahmedabad, India.
